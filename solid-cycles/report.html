Website Refresh Report

Solid Cycles: What is the value of this refresh?

This report explains the reasoning behind the Solid Cycles concept. The objective is to preserve the brand's identity and craftsmanship credibility while improving clarity, trust, and conversion performance.

Preface: The Higher Purpose

This work is about more than visual polish. Clear and purposeful content helps the right customers self-identify faster, understand value with less friction, and take action with confidence. Better clarity creates better conversations, better conversations create better opportunities, and better opportunities improve revenue quality over time.

What We Preserved

  • Hard-edged custom V-twin identity and tone
  • Core value: engines, drivetrain, and fabrication expertise
  • Frank Jezek story and trust-building heritage
  • Dealer and partner references

What We Improved

  • Stronger hierarchy from hero to CTA
  • Cleaner service segmentation for quick scanning
  • Improved mobile readability and tap targets
  • Faster path to contact and pricing action

Reasoning By Section

1. Hero Positioning

The refreshed hero brings specialization and capability to the top of the page so first-time visitors immediately know what Solid Cycles does and why it matters.

2. Services Architecture

Engine/drivetrain, maintenance, and fabrication are separated into clear blocks so buyers can self-identify fit quickly.

3. Proof and Trust

The build gallery, Frank's background, and dealer network are arranged to reinforce authority and reduce decision friction.

4. Conversion Path

Contact and pricing actions are surfaced earlier and repeated at natural intent points to increase inquiry completion.

Expected Commercial Impact

This refresh is designed to improve qualified lead capture by increasing message clarity and reducing navigation effort. Post-launch, impact should be measured via contact click-through rate, call/email initiation, and lead quality trends.
